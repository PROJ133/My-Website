About Us

Our Mission

At Big Brew Coffee Shop, we are dedicated to serving high-quality, handcrafted beverages that cater to all taste preferences. From expertly brewed coffees to refreshing milk teas and fruit teas, our drinks are made with the finest ingredients at prices that won’t break the bank. Our mission is simple: to provide an enjoyable and affordable café experience for everyone.

Our Story

Founded in 2019, Big Brew Coffee Shop is a proudly Filipino-owned brand committed to making delicious, high-quality drinks accessible to all. Over the years, we have built a reputation for excellence by combining premium flavors, quick service, and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're looking for a morning boost, an afternoon treat, or a cozy place to unwind, Big Brew is your go-to destination.

Our Values

We believe in three core principles: quality, affordability, and exceptional service. Every drink we serve is crafted with care, ensuring that each sip delivers the perfect balance of flavor and freshness. Our team is dedicated to creating a fast, friendly, and clean environment where every customer feels valued.

Big Brew Franchise

Big Brew is more than just a coffee shop—it’s a thriving franchise that continues to expand across the Philippines. Led by CEO and founder Rolando Garcia, Big Brew offers entrepreneurs the opportunity to be part of a growing brand known for its affordability and excellence.

Why Choose Big Brew?

  • A diverse menu featuring coffee, milk tea, fruit tea, and snacks
  • Budget-friendly pricing without compromising quality
  • Fast, courteous service in a clean and welcoming setting
  • A commitment to social responsibility and community support

Experience the perfect blend of taste, quality, and affordability at Big Brew Coffee Shop. Visit us today and discover why we are a favorite among coffee and tea lovers!
